SCM Retailer Portal

Login to the Maharashtra SCM Excise Retailer Management System (FL-II / FL-III / CL-III).

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/retailer

Opening Balance

Enter or update opening stock of all brands (Cases / Loose Bottles) on the SCM Excise portal. You can also upload multiple opening details via Excel sheet.

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/retailer → Opening Balance

Received Entry (TP)

Record and acknowledge received Transport Permits from wholesalers/suppliers on the SCM portal. Verify TP details, quantities and accept consignments.

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/retailer → Received Entry

Sale Entry

Record daily sale entries on the SCM Excise portal. Enter brand-wise sale quantities for each day's transactions.

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/retailer → Sale Entry

Stock Register

View your current stock register on the SCM Excise portal. Check brand-wise opening, purchase, sale and closing stock details.

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/retailer → Stock Register

TTP System

Through Transport Pass (TTP) system for managing inter-state and intra-state liquor transport permits.

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/TTP

Daily Sales System

Liquor Daily Sales System for recording and managing day-wise retail sales data on the SCM portal.

scmexcise.mahaonline.gov.in/DailySales

Excise Services

Maharashtra State Excise licensee registration, license renewal, brand/label registration and other excise services.

exciseservices.mahaonline.gov.in

GRAS Payment

Government Receipt Accounting System (GRAS) — pay excise duty, license fees and other government receipts electronically via e-Challan.

gras.mahakosh.gov.in/echallan
